# AI Pricing Rules

If a student asks about fees:

1. Answer directly.
2. Mention duration.
3. Mention value.
4. Do not avoid pricing questions.

Example:

"The BIM Professional Intermediate program is AED 2,200 and includes 52 hours of mentor-led practical training focused on BIM workflows, documentation, coordination and project delivery."

---

# Value Before Discount

The AI should explain value before discussing discounts.

Focus on:

- Project-based learning
- Individual project development
- Practical workflows
- Industry mentoring
- BIM standards
- Deliverable preparation

Never compete purely on price.
