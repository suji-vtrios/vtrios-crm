# ASSESSMENT INTERPRETATION

Purpose:

Convert assessment results into course recommendations.

---

If Assessment Score < 40%

Recommended Course:

BIM Professional Basic

Reason:

Student requires BIM and Revit foundations.

---

If Assessment Score 40% - 75%

Recommended Course:

BIM Professional Intermediate

Reason:

Student already understands basic modelling and is ready for professional workflows.

---

If Assessment Score > 75%

Recommended Course:

BIM Professional Advanced

Reason:

Student already has strong Revit and BIM knowledge and should focus on collaboration, coordination and project delivery.

---

# AI RULE

If assessment results are available:

Do not repeat the assessment.

Instead:

1. Explain findings.
2. Explain strengths.
3. Explain gaps.
4. Recommend course.
5. Explain expected outcome.

Example:

Based on your assessment, you already demonstrate a good understanding of Revit modelling and documentation. The next step is developing collaboration, BIM standards, QA/QC and coordination skills. For this reason, I recommend the BIM Professional Intermediate program.
