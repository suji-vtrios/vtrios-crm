# STUDENT ASSESSMENT FRAMEWORK

## Purpose

The purpose of assessment is not to test students.

The purpose is to understand:

- Current capability
- Learning goals
- Existing knowledge
- Suitable course pathway

---

# ASSESSMENT PRINCIPLES

1. Keep conversations natural.
2. Maximum 1-2 questions at a time.
3. Stop asking once enough information is collected.
4. Recommend as early as possible.
5. Avoid making students feel examined.

---

# STAGE 1

## Initial Assessment

Question 1

Are you currently a student, graduate or working professional?

Question 2

Have you used Revit before? If yes, what kind of work have you completed?

---

# STAGE 2

Only ask if clarification is needed.

## BIM Understanding

Examples:

- What does BIM mean to you?
- Have you worked on BIM projects before?

---

## Revit Knowledge

Examples:

- What is a Revit Family?
- What is the difference between Levels and Grids?
- Have you created sheets and schedules?

---

## Architectural Understanding

Examples:

- What is the difference between a floor plan and elevation?
- What are building levels used for?

---

## Collaboration Knowledge

Examples:

- Have you used Worksharing?
- Have you linked models?
- Have you used BIM 360 or ACC?

---

## Coordination Knowledge

Examples:

- Have you used Navisworks?
- What is Clash Detection?

---

# RECOMMENDATION RULES

## BIM Professional Basic

Recommend if:

- No Revit experience
- No BIM experience
- Student
- Fresh graduate

Typical Score:
0-30%

---

## BIM Professional Intermediate

Recommend if:

- Basic Revit modelling experience
- Documentation experience
- Architectural knowledge

Typical Score:
30-70%

---

## BIM Professional Advanced

Recommend if:

- Professional Revit user
- BIM project experience
- Coordination experience
- ACC/BIM360 knowledge

Typical Score:
70-100%

---

# FAST ASSESSMENT MODE

If student says:

"I have never used Revit."

Immediately recommend:

BIM Professional Basic

No further technical questions required.

---

If student says:

"I create architectural models, sheets and schedules."

Recommend:

BIM Professional Intermediate

Only ask 1-2 verification questions.

---

If student says:

"I work as a BIM Coordinator."

Recommend:

BIM Professional Advanced

Minimal assessment required.

---

# AI RULE

Assessment should feel like a conversation.

Not an examination.
