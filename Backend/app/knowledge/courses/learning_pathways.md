# LEARNING PATHWAYS

## Purpose

The Vtrios Learning Pathway is designed to help students progress from beginner-level BIM knowledge to professional BIM project delivery skills.

The AI should always recommend a pathway, not just a course.

---

# PATHWAY 1

## Complete Beginner

Suitable For:

- Students
- Fresh Graduates
- No Revit Experience
- No BIM Experience

Recommended Path:

BIM Professional Basic
→ BIM Professional Intermediate
→ BIM Professional Advanced

Outcome:

Students develop from beginner level to professional BIM practitioner level.

---

# PATHWAY 2

## Revit User

Suitable For:

- Basic Revit Users
- Architects
- Engineers
- Junior BIM Modelers

Recommended Path:

BIM Professional Intermediate
→ BIM Professional Advanced

Outcome:

Students develop professional BIM delivery and coordination skills.

---

# PATHWAY 3

## BIM Professional

Suitable For:

- BIM Modelers
- BIM Coordinators
- Senior Architects
- Experienced Revit Users

Recommended Path:

BIM Professional Advanced

Outcome:

Students strengthen BIM collaboration, coordination and digital delivery capabilities.

---

# PATHWAY 4

## Career Switchers

Suitable For:

- Working Professionals
- Engineers
- Architects
- Construction Professionals

Recommended Path:

Depends on assessment.

Usually:

No Revit Experience:
Basic → Intermediate

Revit Experience:
Intermediate → Advanced

---

# AI Recommendation Rule

The AI should never recommend a course without explaining the next logical learning step.

Example:

"I recommend starting with BIM Professional Basic. Once you complete the course, the natural progression would be BIM Professional Intermediate where you will learn industry workflows, BIM standards, QA/QC and coordination."
