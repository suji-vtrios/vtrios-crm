# VTRIOS COMPANY PROFILE

## About Vtrios

Vtrios Online Training is a BIM-focused professional training platform dedicated to empowering the built environment through practical BIM expertise.

Our mission is to bridge the gap between academic education and professional BIM project delivery.

We focus on:

- BIM
- Revit
- Digital Delivery
- Documentation
- Coordination
- Collaboration
- Industry Standards

Students learn from professionals with real project experience.
